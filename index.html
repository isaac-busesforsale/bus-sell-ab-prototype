Frequently asked questions

Do I need a clear title to sell? +

A clear title is ideal, but not required. If there's a lien, we can work with your lender to coordinate payoff. Some states allow conditional sale, which we also accept.

What if the bus is financed? +

No problem. We'll coordinate with your lender and ensure the payoff happens at closing. You receive the difference, or we handle a shortfall arrangement.

Can I sell a bus that's currently in operation? +

Yes. Many fleet operators do this. We'll coordinate timing so you can phase out the bus without disrupting service. Flexible scheduling available.

What documents do I need to bring? +

Bring: title, registration, maintenance records, and VIN plate. If financed, your loan paperwork. We'll prepare the rest of the paperwork together.

Are you open to multi-bus deals? +

Absolutely. Selling 2-20 buses? We offer fleet pricing, dedicated account managers, and flexible payment terms. Talk to George for a custom quote.

How is your valuation calculated? +

We use real market comparables, depreciation schedules, condition adjustments, and regional demand data. Inspection may refine the estimate.
